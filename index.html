Safari Tour

Yala & Bundala

We offer Safari tours in Yala, Udawalawa and Bundala National Parks, which are amongst the best eco-tourism destinations in Sri Lanka. If you are a true nature lover and adventure enthusiast, we promise to give you an unforgettable safari experience with safety and no trouble.

You can observe freely roaming leopards, elephants, bears, pigs and many other wild animals in Yala and Udawalawe. And also a wide variety of endemic bird species can be witnessed in travelling through Bundala National Park. Our experienced and friendly drivers will take care of your every need throughout the journey and help you to make an adventurous yet an unforgettable safari ride.
